## Introduction

We know the equation for linear regression:

$$
y = X\beta + \epsilon
$$

Where y is the regressor variable, X is the data, $$ \beta $$ is the parameter and $$ \epsilon $$ is the error term.

**Assumption:**

$$
\epsilon ~ N(0, \sigma^2)
$$

$$
y_i ~ N(\beta^T x_i, \sigma^2)
$$
